# Bushido: The Ethics of the AI Prompt Engineer

![Neo-Edo Cyberpunk Samurai Osushi-chan](/bushido-ethics-in-ai.png)

When we wield the immense, world-bending power of modern Artificial Intelligence, we are no longer just users; we are commanders of a digital legion. In feudal Japan, those who possessed such disproportionate martial power were bound by a strict, unyielding moral code: *Bushido* (武士道), the Way of the Warrior. Today, the prompt engineer must resurrect this code.

## The Weight of the Keystroke

To possess power without ethics is to invite chaos. *Bushido* demands absolute honesty (*Makoto* - 誠) and profound responsibility (*Gi* - 義). When you generate an image, write a script, or automate a decision, are you acting with integrity? The Samurai understood that a sword drawn in malice poisons the wielder. An AI prompt executed with deceptive or lazy intent poisons the entire digital ecosystem.
