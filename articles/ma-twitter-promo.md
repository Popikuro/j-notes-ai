## Post B (The Philosophical Hook)
*(Gentle & Approachable Tone)*

A "Digital Detox" sounds wonderful until... Monday morning arrives. 🫠

Instead of running away to the woods for a week to throw your phone in a river, try practicing **Ma** (間) instead. 

*Ma* is the Japanese concept of "negative space." It’s about building tiny, intentional pauses into your workflow, rather than trying to escape your workflow entirely. 

Close your eyes. Take a 3-second breath before opening the next tab. Sometimes the most productive thing you can do is simply allow space. ☁️🍵
